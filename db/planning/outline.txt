Scoring for ratings will be FantasyPros PPR scoring settings.

Formula is based on top 300 players. Average league is 10 players with 16 roster spots. 300 players in DB should account for even large leagues.

Y = player rating from 0.05 - 15
X = Player ranking according to fantasypros rest of season rankings

Formula:
Y = 15 - ((x-1) * 0.05)
